Launching and Unveiling of NatCA


The National Communications Authority (NatCA) which is
the Electronic Communications Regulatory body in Sierra
Leone was officially launched and unveiled on 15th
December 2023.

The National Communications Authority Acts
2022.



HE President Julius Maada Bio has approved the new
Act to establish the National Communications Authority
and to provide for the licensing and regulation of
electronic communications operators and other related
matters, thereby transforming the Commission into an
Authority.

Amara Brewah appointed Director-General of
NatCA

The Board, Management and Staff of the National
Communications Authority-NatCA
heartily welcomes Mr. Amara Brewah as the new Director-
General of the authority. Until his appointment, Mr. Amara
Brewah was the Deputy Director-General.
